About Me

My name is Ben Chan. From a young age, I've been interested in technology. After graduating high school, I went to college for electrical engineering.
While there, I was required to take a basic computer science course and discovered I liked computer science more than I knew. After a few years, I realized
I wasn't really interested in electrical engineering at all and I ended up dropping out. After a few years of not going to school and working as a tutor for math
and english at an educational network called Kumon, I decided I wanted to work on getting my degree to further my career. I discovered Make School via a
Reddit AMA and have since started my journey to hopefully becoming a software engineer here at Make School.
